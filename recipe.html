The Regenerator

(formula of futurist Ing.Barosi)
  • An egg yolk
  • Half a cup of Asti sparkling wine
  • Three toasted nuts
  • Three teaspoons of sugar

Directions

  1. Beat the whole thing for 10 minutes
  2. Serve in a glass with a banana peeled and protruding inside
from La cucina futurista
